optical illusions at hidn.art

Since the time of the ancient Greeks, illusion art has been practised. Skilled artists realised they could 'trick' viewers into perceiving 2-dimensional artworks, drawn or painted onto flat surfaces, as 3-dimensional people or objects.

Over time, optical illusions in art have become more sophisticated and self-referential. Over the last hundred years in particular, many 'illusionary' artworks have been produced to explore the limitations and possibilities of human powers of perception and psychology.
